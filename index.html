A Vessel of Ancestral Grace

Gogo Spiwe Kubheka is a gifted traditional spiritual healer and Gobela with years of sacred practice, devoted to guiding individuals through spiritual awakening, emotional healing, and ancestral reconnection.

As a Gobela, He trains and initiates those called by the ancestors into the sacred path of ukuthwasa, providing mentorship, ceremony, and lifelong spiritual support.
